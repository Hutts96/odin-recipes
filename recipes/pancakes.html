Pancakes

Description

While these pancakes may not be the fluffy ones you're used to, they are definitely worth a try. This recipe is originally my grandmothers, who passed it down to my mother, who passed it down to me and now I am sharing it with you.

Ingredients

  • 400 grams flour
  • 550 grams milk
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 tablespoons sunflower-seed oil
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl add the eggs, sugar, salt, oil and milk then mix
  2. Slowly add flour to bowl while mixing. Do NOT add it all at once to prevent clumps.
  3. In a nonstick pan over medium heat add butter and let it melt
  4. Once the butter starts bubbling add pancake batter with a ladle to the pan
  5. Once the top of the pancake is solid flip it over
  6. Once both sides are a dark brown transfer to a plate
  7. Repeat from step 4 util no more batter is left
